Welcome

Hi my name is Naomi Thompson and I'm a young female student with a passion for software & game development.

I love taking an idea from conception to completion. I like to describe it as digital midwifery. I grow and develop the idea and when it's complete I deliver the idea.

I'm a semi-self taught developer with the majority of a bachelor's degree in information technology under my belt.

My favourite art style is pixel art and I make games as a hobby that I wish to turn professionally. I'm interested in gaining further experience, learning and making the best games I possibly can, whether they're for myself or another.
